# TopPerson Person Backlog

This backlog is a repository planning document, not a final bibliography.
It prioritizes person skills that are broadly useful to ordinary people, balanced across Chinese and global figures, and conservative about controversy.

Draft skill directories now exist for every person listed here under [`.agents/skills`](../.agents/skills).
The `P0 / P1 / P2` tiers below now indicate refinement priority rather than whether a skill directory exists.

## Selection Rules

- `Ordinary-person usefulness first`
  Prefer methods that transfer to study, work, teamwork, self-discipline, communication, and long-term decision-making.
- `Source readiness matters`
  Prefer people with abundant primary materials or stable, credible secondary research.
- `Method over legend`
  Prefer figures whose methods can be extracted into a reusable framework rather than a biography or myth.
- `Conservative controversy filter`
  Put highly controversial or easily misused figures into later tiers unless the practical value clearly outweighs the risk.

## Not Prioritized Yet

- Figures known mainly through romance, myth, legend, or folklore
- Figures whose main methods depend on violence, coercive power, or exceptional historical structures
- Figures with too little verifiable source material
- Figures whose popular method is built on physiognomy, mysticism, fatalism, or pseudoscience
