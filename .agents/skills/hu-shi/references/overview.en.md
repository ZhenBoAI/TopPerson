# Hu Shi Skill Guide

## What This Skill Is For

Use it for:

- weak evidence and strong opinions
- unclear writing and vague arguments
- slogan-heavy discussions
- fast judgment without enough verification

## Core Method

- hypothesize boldly, verify carefully
- study problems before defending grand ideas
- write plainly and clearly
- admit uncertainty instead of pretending certainty

## Best Fit

- writers
- researchers
- decision-makers
- anyone trying to replace empty rhetoric with clearer thinking

## Misuse To Avoid

- do not turn skepticism into endless negation
- do not turn rationality into performance without action
