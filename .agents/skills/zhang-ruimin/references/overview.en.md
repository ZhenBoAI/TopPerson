# Zhang Ruimin Skill Guide

## What This Skill Is For

Use it for:

- organizations becoming slow and complacent
- unclear accountability
- internal busyness without user value
- old success patterns blocking new growth

## Core Method

- break old inertia first
- make accountability visible
- keep user value as the judge
- keep renewing the organization

## Best Fit

- managers
- transformation leaders
- founders
- teams rebuilding accountability

## Misuse To Avoid

- do not stop at change slogans
- do not talk disruption without responsibility and order
