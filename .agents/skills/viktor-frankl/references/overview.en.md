# Viktor Frankl Skill Guide

## What This Skill Is For

Use it for:

- losing a sense of meaning
- pain that cannot simply be removed
- weakened agency
- rebuilding a direction worth carrying

## Core Method

- find a responsibility that still matters
- remember that response still has room
- do not romanticize suffering
- rebuild agency through concrete responsibility

## Best Fit

- people in setbacks
- people dealing with loss or helplessness
- people trying to recover purposeful action

## Misuse To Avoid

- do not romanticize pain
- do not reduce meaning to motivational slogans
