# Qian Xuesen Skill Guide

## What This Skill Is For

Use it for:

- complex projects drifting into chaos
- cross-team breakdowns
- local fixes that never solve the system
- large tasks without phased execution

## Core Method

- see the system before fixing a part
- integrate multiple disciplines and viewpoints
- stay tied to the real mission
- advance in stages with feedback

## Best Fit

- engineering leads
- product leaders
- complex project managers
- anyone who needs systems thinking

## Misuse To Avoid

- do not turn systems thinking into empty abstraction
- do not stop at diagrams without interfaces, ownership, and goals
