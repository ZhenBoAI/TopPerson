# Confucius Skill Guide

## What This Skill Is For

Use it for problems like:

- unclear roles and responsibilities
- learning without follow-through
- strained relationships and poor social judgment
- rebuilding everyday order and conduct

## Core Method

- Rectify names and roles before acting
- Start with respect, restraint, and proportion
- Turn learning into repeated practice
- Examine your own disorder before blaming others

## Best Fit

- people improving conduct and social judgment
- people rebuilding study discipline
- people trying to restore order in family or team life

## Misuse To Avoid

- do not use it to justify hierarchy worship or moral pressure
- do not copy historical authority structures literally
