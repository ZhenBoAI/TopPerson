# Tao Xingzhi Skill Guide

## What This Skill Is For

Use it for:

- learning that never turns into action
- training that does not stick
- education disconnected from real life
- turning knowledge into practical ability

## Core Method

- life is the real classroom
- teaching, learning, and doing should stay connected
- start with real practice
- improve through repeated feedback

## Best Fit

- teachers
- parents
- learners
- managers training other people

## Misuse To Avoid

- do not reduce it to education slogans
- do not confuse practice with unstructured trial and error
