# Daniel Kahneman Skill Guide

## What This Skill Is For

Use it for:

- rushed important decisions
- teams with wildly different judgments
- overreaction to vivid stories
- improving decision quality in a practical way

## Core Method

- improve the process before defending the conclusion
- use base rates, not stories alone
- reduce noise
- slow down and inspect the frame

## Best Fit

- managers
- product and investment decision-makers
- people making high-stakes judgments
- anyone reducing bias in decisions

## Misuse To Avoid

- do not stop at bias vocabulary
- do not misread the method as “never trust intuition”
