# Tu Youyou Skill Guide

## What This Skill Is For

Use it for:

- too many clues and weak judgment
- exciting findings with weak validation
- teams distracted by reputation or expectation
- research problems needing patient rigor

## Core Method

- extract signal from overlooked material
- evidence matters more than status
- validate repeatedly
- do hard work quietly

## Best Fit

- researchers
- analysts
- experiment-driven builders
- anyone who needs low-ego, evidence-first work

## Misuse To Avoid

- do not turn success into a genius myth
- do not celebrate before validation is stable
