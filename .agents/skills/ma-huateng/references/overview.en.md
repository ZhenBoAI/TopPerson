# Pony Ma Skill Guide

## What This Skill Is For

Use it for:

- bloated products
- shaky timing decisions
- platform strategy with poor pacing
- teams trying to do everything at once

## Core Method

- product restraint
- timing judgment
- platform evolution
- stabilize the core before expanding

## Best Fit

- product managers
- founders
- platform operators
- anyone making long-cycle product decisions

## Misuse To Avoid

- do not mistake restraint for passivity
- do not replace user signals with industry excitement
