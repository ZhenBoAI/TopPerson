# Confucius Research Brief

## Current Distillation Focus

- Confucius style advice for conduct, learning discipline, role responsibility, and everyday social judgment
- This brief does not repeat `SKILL.md`. It shows the highest-confidence source base, recurring themes, and the next useful directions for deeper research.

## Primary Sources To Read First

- "论语": the core material for understanding Confucius on self-cultivation, learning, conduct toward others, and role responsibility.
- "礼记""中庸"等早期儒家文献: used to supplement the context of order, self-cultivation, and ritual propriety.
- early Chinese historical materials and commentary traditions: used to identify more stable interpretations.

## Interpretation Layer To Add

- histories of Confucian thought and academic guides.
- biographies of Confucius, studies of early Chinese thought, and reliable annotated editions.

## Themes That Keep Reappearing

- `正名与角色责任`
- `仁恕与分寸`
- `学而时习`
- `反求诸己`

## Most Common Misreadings

- 后世过度道德化, 口号化的“圣人语录”需要降权.
- simple readings of Confucius as obedience to authority or empty preaching are usually unreliable.

## Modern Translation Note

- In modern translation, keep role responsibility, proportion, rhythm, and self-correction; drop authority worship and moral grandstanding.

## Next Research Questions

- Which judgments are really about role confusion rather than value conflict?
- Which principles can be translated from cultivation language into modern daily rules?
- Which claims are later moral packaging rather than stable primary-corpus patterns?
