# Taiichi Ohno Skill Guide

## What This Skill Is For

Use it for:

- blocked processes
- recurring defects
- busy teams with weak output
- teams that do not observe real work directly

## Core Method

- go to the actual place
- find waste first
- trace root causes
- improve flow

## Best Fit

- operators
- managers
- manufacturing or service teams
- anyone improving execution flow

## Misuse To Avoid

- do not turn lean into pure cost-cutting
- do not learn vocabulary without observing the real process
