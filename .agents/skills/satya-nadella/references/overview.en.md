# Satya Nadella Skill Guide

## What This Skill Is For

Use it for:

- rigid culture
- smart teams that exhaust each other
- organizations trapped in past success
- renewal that should not rely on fear and pressure

## Core Method

- empathy is not weakness
- move from know-it-all to learn-it-all
- culture must become behavior
- renewal is a long process

## Best Fit

- managers
- team leads
- transformation builders
- leaders trying to combine standards with humanity

## Misuse To Avoid

- do not mistake empathy for lowered standards
- do not leave culture change at the slogan level
